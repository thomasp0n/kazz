Biography

A filmmaker driven by stories that matter.

KAZZ is a filmmaker, director, and writer based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. With a deep passion for visual storytelling, he creates work that spans music videos, short films, commercials, and documentaries.

His projects reflect a commitment to authentic, bold narratives — every frame crafted with intention, driven by curiosity, culture, and the belief that cinema can spark change. From intimate stories to large-scale productions, KAZZ brings a distinctive cinematic vision to everything he touches.
